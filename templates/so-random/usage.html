Automated Form Filling - Instructions

A script has been copied to the clipboard that contains your generated numbers. To purchase a lotto ticket using these numbers follow the steps below.

Navigate to the ozlotteries powerball website and select your number of games from the dropdown. Make sure that the number of games you select is enough for the number of lines you generated on the previous page.

Select the 'Choose your numbers' checkbox.

Press the F12 key on your keyboard.

At the cursor next to the blue arrow, right click and select paste.

Press the Enter key.

Now type autoFill(); and press the Enter key.

Once that has completed your numbers should automatically fill the form.
